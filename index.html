Investing for Web 3.0

sire and relic are designed for Investment apps

sire itself is an investment, generating relic as the Ethereum network moves forward. In addition to that sire and relic will play a pivotal role in a larger ecosystem with a wider variety of traditional investment vehicles being offered by the sire development team.

sire's contract development team during the end of Phase One and Start of Phase Two will begin working on implementing traditional investment schemes that utilize both sire and relic.

sire & relic Tokens

Backed by one of the worlds largest Cryptocurrencies: Ethereum

sire introduces three new tradable assets. Two ERC20 tokens known as: sire and relic, and an abstract asset known as Mint. The sire smart contract has has several functions: a create Mint function that enables you to mint a specified amount of relic per block based on the amount of sire you lock within the Mint.

sire, relic and Mint's become speculative assets with a relationship dependent on a large number of factors: amount of Ether collected and locked in the contract during ICO, current fiat value of Ether, maximum sire in circulation, and maximum relic in circulation.

Decentralized sire Exchange

Swap: sire, relic, Ethereum, Mint's, and ERC20 tokens on the sire Decentralized exchange

The sire development team has plans to introduce a Decentralized Exchange specifically designed for: sire, relic, Ethereum, Mint's and ERC20 Tokens.

The sire exchange contract will enable users to freely trade between a multitude of currencies. In Phase three of our roadmap we will work to enable many popular ERC20 tokens for exchange as well.
